# Bounties

**Funa ssente nga okola tricks!** SkateHive Bounties ekusobozesa okutonda challenge za tricks ne ofuna emipeera mu crypto ku blockchain zombi.

🎯 **Laba bounties:** [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)

---

## 🌐 Enkola Bbiri eza Blockchain

SkateHive ewagira bounties ku network zombi ebbiri:

### Bounties za Hive
- **Ssente:** HBD oba HIVE
- **Platform:** Blockchain ya Hive
- **Okusasula:** Eyakitonda akakasa abawanguzi manually
- **Esingawo ku:** Challenge za community, emipeera ya social

### Bounties za POIDH (Ethereum)
- **Ssente:** ETH
- **Platform:** Base oba Arbitrum (Layer 2)
- **Okusasula:** Smart contract (okukebera on-chain)
- **Esingawo ku:** Okukakasa decentralized, emipeera ETH

## 💎 Engeri y'Okutonda Bounty

### Omutendera 1: Nyiga "+ CREATE BOUNTY"

Ku muko gwa bounties ogw'okusooka, nyiga button **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"** (ku corner waggulu ku ddyo).

### Omutendera 2: Londa Blockchain Yo

![Londa Blockchain](/img/bounties/02-choose-blockchain.png)

Ojja kulaba ebirowoozo bibiri:

- **HIVE** (kkono) — Sasula mu HBD oba HIVE
- **ETH** (ddyo) — Sasula on-chain via POIDH

---

### Okutonda Bounty ya Hive

![Form ya Hive](/img/bounties/03-hive-form.png)

**Ebyeetaagisa:**
- Extension ya Hive Keychain etereezeddwa
- Account ne posting key

**Jjuza form:**

1. **Challenge** — Ennyonnyola empimpi ya trick (okugeza: "Kickflip ku madaala 5")
2. **Rules** — Ebyeetaagisa mu bujjuvu (supports markdown)
   - Ekyokulabirako: "Kirina okuba ku ttaka, tewali kutemamu, yogera 'this is for skatehive' nga tonnakola trick"
3. **Upload media** (optional):
   - **Upload Image** — Thumbnail ya bounty
   - **Upload Video** — Video ya reference
4. **Reward:**
   - Wandiika omuwendo
   - Londa ssente (dropdown HBD oba HIVE)
5. **Deadline** (optional) — Londa olunaku okutandisa
6. **POST BOUNTY** — Kakasa ne popup ya Hive Keychain

**✅ Kiwedde!** Bounty yo etandika instant.

**Engabanya y'Empeera (Hive):**
- 1 omuwanguzi: 100%
- 2 abawanguzi: 70% / 30%
- 3 abawanguzi: 50% / 30% / 20%

---

### Okutonda Bounty ya POIDH

**Ebyeetaagisa:**
- Wallet erimu ETH ku Base oba Arbitrum
- MetaMask, Rainbow oba wallet ekwatagana

**Emitendera:**

1. Nyiga **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"** → Londa **"ETH"**
2. **Yungisa wallet** (singa teyungizibbwa)
3. Jjuza form:
   - **Bounty name** — Omutwe omufupi
   - **Description** — Ebyeetaagisa ku trick
   - **Reward** — Omuwendo mu ETH
   - **Chain** — Base (gas etono) oba Arbitrum
4. **Create Bounty** → Kakasa transaction mu wallet
5. Lindirira ~30 seconds ku indexer

**✅ Kiwedde!** Bounty erabika ku list 🚀

:::tip Misolo ya Gas
**Base** erina misolo emikalu (~$0.01-0.05). **Arbitrum** nayo mikalu naye mtonnyeko.
:::

## 🏆 Engeri y'Okusaba Bounty

### Okusaba Bounty ya Hive

![Detail ya Bounty ya Hive](/img/bounties/05-hive-detail.png)

**Emitendera:**

1. **Nyiga card** → Omuko gw'ebikwata gujja kuggulawo
2. Scroll okutuuka ku **"CLAIM THIS BOUNTY TO SUBMIT YOUR PROOF"**
3. **Nyiga "CLAIM IT"**
4. Jjuza form:
   - **Title** — Erinnya lya submission yo
   - **Description** — Link ya video (YouTube, Streamable, IPFS)
   - **Upload proof** — Video oba ekifaananyi eky'okulaga trick
5. **Sign n'ekikozesebwa Hive Keychain**
6. **Lindirira okukebezebwa kw'eyakitonda** — Ddayo ku muko gwa bounty

**Bounties za Hive zisasulwa eyakitonda bw'akakasa claim yo manually.**

---

### Okusaba Bounty ya POIDH

![Detail ya Bounty ya POIDH](/img/bounties/04-poidh-detail.png)

**Emitendera:**

1. **Yungisa wallet** (corner waggulu ku ddyo)
2. **Nyiga card** → Omuko gw'ebikwata gujja kuggulawo
3. **Nyiga "SUBMIT PROOF"** (wansi)
4. Jjuza form:
   - **Claim name** — Ennyonnyola empimpi
   - **Description** — Link ya video oba upload
   - **Attach media** — IPFS upload automatic
5. **Kakasa transaction** mu wallet
6. **Omutendera gw'okulonda** (singa enabled) oba okukakasa instant
7. **Funa** claim bwe kikakasibwa! 💸

**Bounties za POIDH zisasulwa automatic via smart contract.**

## 💰 Engeri Okusasula Gy'ekola

### Okusasula kwa Hive
- **Enkola:** Transferi za Hive (automatic oluvannyuma lw'okukakasa)
- **Kebera:** Wallet yo ya Hive (Keychain oba [PeakD](https://peakd.com))

### Okusasula kwa POIDH
- **Enkola:** Smart contract (instant nga kikakasiddwa)
- **Kebera:** Wallet yo eyungiddwa
- **Laba ku:** Blockchain explorer (Basescan, Arbiscan)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Nsobola okusaba bounty yange?**  
R: Nedda — bounties za ba-skaters abalala okuzikolera.

**Q: Bbanga ki okutuusa lwe nfuna ssente?**  
R: **Hive:** Eyakitonda bw'akakasa claim yo  
**POIDH:** Okulonda bwe kuwanguka oba instant (kyesigamye ku config)

**Q: Singa claim yange esibiddwa?**  
R: Gezaako nate singa bounty ekyali open. Kebera amateeka otereeze submission yo.

**Q: Nsobola okusazaamu bounty?**  
R: **Hive:** Kyusa metadata ya post okuteeka nga closed  
**POIDH:** Kozesa function ya cancel mu contract (ETH ekomawo minus gas)

**Q: Njawulo ki eri wakati wa Hive ne POIDH?**  
R: **Hive** = blockchain ya social, okukakasa manually, omuzira gwa community  
**POIDH** = smart contracts za Ethereum, okukakasa on-chain, okusasula automatic

**Q: Blockchain ki gy'ondikozesa?**  
R: **Hive** ku community engagement  
**POIDH** ku ba Ethereum users n'okukakasa decentralized

**Q: Nsobola okutonda bounties ennyingi?**  
R: Yee, tewali nkomo ku platform zombi!

**Q: Ssente ki ezikola?**  
R: **Hive:** HBD oba HIVE zokka  
**POIDH:** ETH yokka (ku Base oba Arbitrum)

**Q: Singa tewali akola trick?**  
R: **Hive:** Osigala ne crypto yo (golola deadline oba post nate)  
**POIDH:** Cancel bounty ofune ETH (minus gas)
