## ğŸ¦ SkateBank (HBD Savings)

SkateBank is Skatehive's savings section where you earn **15% APR** on your HBD:

### How It Works

1. Go to [skatehive.app/wallet](https://skatehive.app/wallet)
2. Navigate to the **SkateBank** tab
3. You'll see:
   - Your current HBD Savings balance
   - Available liquid HBD to invest
   - Estimated claimable interest
   - Days until next interest claim

### Key Features

- **15% Annual Interest**: Guaranteed returns on your HBD savings
- **Monthly Interest Claims**: Claim your earned interest
- **3-Day Withdrawal Period**: Savings withdrawals take 3 days for security
- **Pending Withdrawals**: See any active withdrawal requests

### Actions

- **Add to Savings**: Move liquid HBD into savings to start earning
- **Withdraw from Savings**: Initiate a 3-day withdrawal back to liquid HBD
