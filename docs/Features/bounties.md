# Bounties

**Get paid to land tricks!** SkateHive Bounties is a dual-blockchain system where skaters create trick challenges and earn crypto rewards.

🎯 **View bounties:** [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)

---

## 🌐 Two Blockchain Systems

### Hive Bounties
- **Currency:** HBD or HIVE
- **Platform:** Hive blockchain (social layer)
- **Claims:** Post replies with video
- **Approval:** Creator picks winners manually

### POIDH Bounties (Ethereum)
- **Currency:** ETH
- **Platform:** Base or Arbitrum (Ethereum L2s)
- **Claims:** On-chain via smart contract
- **Approval:** Voting or instant (depends on bounty config)

:::tip
Both types appear together on [/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties) — filter by **ALL / HIVE / POIDH** tabs.
:::

---

## 🎯 How It Works

### For Claimers (Skaters)

1. **Browse bounties** at [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)
2. **Pick a challenge** you want to attempt
3. **Land the trick** and record it
4. **Submit your clip:**
   - **Hive:** Click "CLAIM IT" → Upload proof → Sign with Keychain
   - **POIDH:** Connect wallet → "SUBMIT CLAIM" → Upload → Approve transaction
5. **Get paid** when approved! 💰

### For Creators (Sponsors)

1. **Click "+ CREATE BOUNTY"**
2. **Choose blockchain:**
   - **HIVE:** For social/community rewards
   - **ETH (POIDH):** For Ethereum L2 rewards
3. **Fill details:**
   - Trick description
   - Reward amount
   - Deadline (optional, Hive only)
   - Media (image/video)
4. **Publish** and wait for submissions
5. **Approve winners** and watch them get paid!

## 📝 Create a Hive Bounty

**Prerequisites:**
- Hive Keychain browser extension installed
- Account with posting key

**Steps:**

1. Go to [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)
2. Click **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"**
3. Select **"HIVE"**
4. Fill form:
   - **Title:** Clear trick description (e.g., "Kickflip BS Boardslide")
   - **Description:** Rules, spot details, requirements (supports markdown)
   - **Reward:** Amount + Currency (HBD or HIVE dropdown)
   - **Deadline:** Optional date (creates urgency!)
   - **Image:** Upload thumbnail (optional)
5. Click **"PUBLISH BOUNTY"**
6. Approve with **Hive Keychain** popup
7. Done! Bounty appears instantly 🎉

**Reward Distribution (Hive):**
- **1 winner:** 100%
- **2 winners:** 70% / 30%
- **3 winners:** 50% / 30% / 20%

:::info Example
100 HIVE bounty, 3 winners → 50 / 30 / 20 HIVE each
:::

## 💎 Create a POIDH Bounty

**Prerequisites:**
- Wallet with ETH on **Base** or **Arbitrum** (Ethereum L2s)
- MetaMask, Rainbow, or compatible wallet

**Steps:**

1. Go to [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)
2. Click **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"**
3. Select **"ETH (POIDH)"**
4. **Connect wallet** (if not connected)
5. Fill form:
   - **Bounty name:** Short title
   - **Description:** Trick requirements
   - **Reward:** Amount in ETH
   - **Chain:** Base or Arbitrum (dropdown)
6. Click **"CREATE BOUNTY"**
7. **Approve transaction** in your wallet
8. Wait ~30 seconds for indexer sync
9. Bounty is live! 🚀

:::tip Gas Fees
Use **Base** for lower fees (~$0.01-0.05). Arbitrum is also cheap but slightly higher.
:::

## 🏆 Claim a Bounty

### Hive Bounty Claim

1. **Click bounty card** → Detail page opens
2. **Click "CLAIM IT"** button
3. **Fill claim form:**
   - **Title:** Your claim name (e.g., "Kickflip down 5-stair")
   - **Description:** Video link (YouTube, Streamable, IPFS)
   - **Upload proof:** Video or image
4. **Sign with Hive Keychain**
5. **Wait for creator review** — Check back on the bounty page

### POIDH Bounty Claim

1. **Connect your wallet** (top-right corner)
2. **Click bounty card** → Detail page opens
3. **Click "SUBMIT CLAIM"**
4. **Fill claim form:**
   - **Claim name:** Short description
   - **Description:** Video link or upload
   - **Attach media:** Upload to IPFS automatically
5. **Approve transaction** in wallet
6. **Voting phase** (if enabled) or instant approval
7. **Get paid** when accepted! 💸

## 🔥 Pro Tips

### For Sponsors

✅ **Set clear rules** — Specificity prevents disputes  
✅ **Choose fair rewards** — Higher = more participants  
✅ **Engage with claims** — Comment, encourage skaters  
✅ **Pay promptly** — Don't leave winners waiting

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I claim my own bounty?**  
A: No — bounties are for other skaters to complete.

**Q: How long until I get paid?**  
A: **Hive:** When creator approves your claim | **POIDH:** When vote passes or instantly if no voting.

**Q: What if my claim is rejected?**  
A: You can try again if the bounty is still open. Review the rules and improve your submission.

**Q: Can I cancel a bounty?**  
A: **Hive:** Edit post metadata to mark as closed | **POIDH:** Use cancel function in contract (returns your ETH minus gas).

**Q: What's the difference between Hive and POIDH?**  
A: **Hive** = social blockchain with community feel, HBD/HIVE rewards, manual approval. **POIDH** = Ethereum smart contracts, ETH rewards, on-chain voting/instant approval.

**Q: Which blockchain should I use?**  
A: **Hive** for community engagement and social rewards. **POIDH** for Ethereum-native users and decentralized verification.

**Q: Can I sponsor multiple bounties?**  
A: Yes, unlimited on both platforms!

**Q: What currencies work?**  
A: **Hive:** HBD or HIVE only | **POIDH:** ETH only (on Base or Arbitrum).

**Q: What if no one lands the trick?**  
A: **Hive:** You keep your crypto. Extend deadline or repost. | **POIDH:** Cancel the bounty to get your ETH back.
