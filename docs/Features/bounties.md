## ğŸ’° Creating a Bounty

Want to challenge the community? Here's how to create a bounty:

### Step 1: Plan Your Challenge

Decide on:
- **The trick** - What do you want to see landed?
- **The spot** - Specific location or any spot?
- **The reward** - How much HIVE/HBD?
- **Duration** - How long will the bounty run?

### Step 2: Create the Bounty

1. Go to [skatehive.app](https://skatehive.app) and find the **Bounties** section
2. Click **"Create Bounty"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Clear description of the trick
   - **Description**: Rules, requirements, tips
   - **Reward**: Amount to pay the winner
   - **Deadline**: When submissions close
4. Fund the bounty with your tokens
5. Publish!

### Example Bounty

```
Title: Kickflip the 5-stair at Plaza Central
Reward: 50 HIVE
Duration: 2 weeks
Rules:
- Must be landed clean
- Film from the side
- Tag #skatehive in your post
```

## ğŸ¬ Submitting to a Bounty

### Step 1: Find a Bounty

Browse active bounties on the bounty page or in the community feed.

### Step 2: Film Your Clip

1. Head to the spot (if specified)
2. Land the trick
3. Get a clean clip

### Step 3: Submit

1. Create a new post with your video
2. In your post, mention the bounty or use the submission button
3. Your submission becomes a "Snap" linked to the bounty
4. The community can vote on your submission

---

## ğŸ—³ï¸ Voting & Winning

### How Voting Works

- Community members upvote submissions they like
- Votes count toward the submission's score
- Higher votes = better chance of winning

### How Winners Are Chosen

The bounty creator has final say:
1. Reviews all submissions
2. Can consider votes, style, creativity
3. Selects the winner
4. Triggers the payout

### Payouts

When the bounty ends:
1. Creator selects the winning submission
2. A winner announcement is posted automatically
3. Reward is transferred to the winner's account

## â“ FAQ

### Can I create multiple bounties?

Yes! Create as many as you want, just fund each one.

### What if no one completes my bounty?

You can extend the deadline or cancel and recover your funds (minus any spent on posted submissions).

### Can I submit to my own bounty?

Generally no - it would be unfair. Create bounties for others to attempt.

### What if there's a tie?

The creator decides. They might split the reward or pick based on other factors.

### How do I know a submission is legit?

Videos must be new and unedited. The community helps call out any suspicious submissions.

### Can the reward increase?

Yes! Others can add to the bounty pool to increase the reward.
