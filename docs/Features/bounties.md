# Bounties

**Get paid to land tricks!** SkateHive Bounties lets you create trick challenges and earn crypto rewards on two blockchains.

🎯 **View bounties:** [skatehive.app/bounties](https://skatehive.app/bounties)

---

## 🌐 Two Blockchain Systems

SkateHive supports bounties on two different networks:

### Hive Bounties
- **Currency:** HBD or HIVE
- **Platform:** Hive blockchain
- **Payment:** Creator manually approves winners
- **Best for:** Community challenges, social rewards

### POIDH Bounties (Ethereum)
- **Currency:** ETH
- **Platform:** Base or Arbitrum (Layer 2)
- **Payment:** Smart contract (on-chain verification)
- **Best for:** Decentralized verification, ETH rewards

## 💎 How to Create a Bounty

### Step 1: Click "+ CREATE BOUNTY"

On the main bounties page, click the **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"** button (top right).

### Step 2: Choose Your Blockchain

![Choose Blockchain](/img/bounties/02-choose-blockchain.png)

You'll see two options:

- **HIVE** (left) — Pay in HBD or HIVE
- **ETH** (right) — Pay on-chain via POIDH

---

### Creating a Hive Bounty

![Hive Form](/img/bounties/03-hive-form.png)

**Requirements:**
- Hive Keychain extension installed
- Account with posting key

**Fill the form:**

1. **Challenge** — Short trick description (e.g., "Kickflip down 5 stair")
2. **Rules** — Detailed requirements (supports markdown)
   - Example: "Must be flat ground, no cuts, say 'this is for skatehive' before trick"
3. **Upload media** (optional):
   - **Upload Image** — Thumbnail for the bounty
   - **Upload Video** — Reference video
4. **Reward:**
   - Enter amount
   - Select currency (HBD or HIVE dropdown)
5. **Deadline** (optional) — Pick a date to create urgency
6. **POST BOUNTY** — Approve with Hive Keychain popup

**✅ Done!** Your bounty goes live instantly.

**Reward Split (Hive):**
- 1 winner: 100%
- 2 winners: 70% / 30%
- 3 winners: 50% / 30% / 20%

---

### Creating a POIDH Bounty

**Requirements:**
- Wallet with ETH on Base or Arbitrum
- MetaMask, Rainbow, or compatible wallet

**Steps:**

1. Click **"+ CREATE BOUNTY"** → Select **"ETH"**
2. **Connect wallet** (if not connected)
3. Fill form:
   - **Bounty name** — Short title
   - **Description** — Trick requirements
   - **Reward** — Amount in ETH
   - **Chain** — Base (cheaper gas) or Arbitrum
4. **Create Bounty** → Approve transaction in wallet
5. Wait ~30 seconds for indexer sync

**✅ Done!** Bounty appears on the list 🚀

:::tip Gas Fees
**Base** has lower fees (~$0.01-0.05). **Arbitrum** is also cheap but slightly higher.
:::

## 🏆 How to Claim a Bounty

### Claiming a Hive Bounty

![Hive Bounty Detail](/img/bounties/05-hive-detail.png)

**Steps:**

1. **Click bounty card** → Detail page opens
2. Scroll down to **"CLAIM THIS BOUNTY TO SUBMIT YOUR PROOF"**
3. **Click "CLAIM IT"** button
4. Fill claim form:
   - **Title** — Your submission name
   - **Description** — Video link (YouTube, Streamable, IPFS)
   - **Upload proof** — Video or image of you landing the trick
5. **Sign with Hive Keychain**
6. **Wait for creator review** — Check back on the bounty page

**Hive bounties are paid after creator manually approves your claim.**

---

### Claiming a POIDH Bounty

![POIDH Bounty Detail](/img/bounties/04-poidh-detail.png)

**Steps:**

1. **Connect wallet** (top-right corner)
2. **Click bounty card** → Detail page opens
3. **Click "SUBMIT PROOF"** button (bottom)
4. Fill claim form:
   - **Claim name** — Short description
   - **Description** — Video link or upload
   - **Attach media** — Upload to IPFS automatically
5. **Approve transaction** in wallet
6. **Voting phase** (if enabled) or instant approval
7. **Get paid** when claim is accepted! 💸

**POIDH bounties are paid automatically via smart contract.**

## 💰 How Payment Works

### Hive Payment
- **Method:** Hive transfers (automatic after approval)
- **Check:** Your Hive wallet (Keychain or [PeakD](https://peakd.com))
- **Shows in:** Transaction history

### POIDH Payment
- **Method:** Smart contract (instant on approval)
- **Check:** Your connected wallet
- **View on:** Blockchain explorer (Basescan, Arbiscan)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I claim my own bounty?**  
A: No — bounties are for other skaters to complete.

**Q: How long until I get paid?**  
A: **Hive:** When creator approves your claim  
**POIDH:** When vote passes or instantly (depends on bounty config)

**Q: What if my claim is rejected?**  
A: Try again if the bounty is still open. Review the rules and improve your submission.

**Q: Can I cancel a bounty?**  
A: **Hive:** Edit post metadata to mark as closed  
**POIDH:** Use cancel function in contract (returns ETH minus gas)

**Q: What's the difference between Hive and POIDH?**  
A: **Hive** = social blockchain, manual approval, community feel  
**POIDH** = Ethereum smart contracts, on-chain verification, automatic payment

**Q: Which blockchain should I use?**  
A: **Hive** for community engagement  
**POIDH** for Ethereum users and decentralized verification

**Q: Can I sponsor multiple bounties?**  
A: Yes, unlimited on both platforms!

**Q: What currencies work?**  
A: **Hive:** HBD or HIVE only  
**POIDH:** ETH only (on Base or Arbitrum)

**Q: What if no one lands the trick?**  
A: **Hive:** You keep your crypto (extend deadline or repost)  
**POIDH:** Cancel bounty to get ETH back (minus gas)
