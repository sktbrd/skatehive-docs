# Account Sponsorship

Upgrade email accounts to full Hive accounts. Community members pay 3 HIVE or 1 ACC to sponsor newcomers.

---

## 🆕 Getting Sponsored

**Requirements:**
- Email account on Skatehive
- Active participation (posts, comments)

**Steps:**

1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Click **Upgrade to Hive Account**
3. Click **Request Sponsorship**
4. Share your link in [Discord #sponsorship](https://discord.gg/skatehive)
5. Wait for approval (usually < 24hrs)

**What you get:**
- ✅ Own Hive account (not shared @skateuser)
- ✅ Earn crypto rewards
- ✅ Vote with your own power
- ✅ Own your content permanently

---

## 🎯 Sponsoring Others

**Requirements:**
- Active Hive account
- 3 HIVE in wallet (~$0.60)
- Hive Keychain installed

**Steps:**

1. Someone shares sponsorship link with you
2. Click link → review request
3. Choose payment method:
   - **Direct:** Transfer 3 HIVE (simple)
   - **RC Delegation:** Use Hive Power (advanced)
4. Approve in Keychain
5. Done! Account created in 24hrs

**Cost breakdown:**
```
Account creation fee:  2.5 HIVE
Initial power-up:      0.5 HIVE
Total:                 3.0 HIVE (~$0.60)
```

**You get:**
- Leaderboard recognition
- "Sponsor" badge
- Karma points 😎

---

## 💡 Tips

**For requestors:**
- Be active before requesting
- Write genuine intro
- Don't spam multiple people
- Thank your sponsor publicly

**For sponsors:**
- Check profile first
- Look for genuine engagement
- Help them get started
- Don't sponsor suspected bots
