**NOT shared:**

### Key Management

When a new account is created:
1. Keys are generated on Hive blockchain
2. Encrypted and sent to new member's email
3. NOT stored on Skatehive servers
4. Member should save keys offline immediately

**Best practices:**
- Write keys on paper
- Store in password manager (1Password, Bitwarden)
- Never share posting/active keys publicly
- Use posting key for daily posting
- Keep active/owner keys for backup only

## â“ FAQ

### How long does sponsorship take?
Account creation is usually instant, but can take up to 24 hours during high demand.

### Can I sponsor multiple people?
Yes! Unlimited. As long as you have HIVE in your wallet.

### What if the person never uses their account?
The HIVE is already spent, but you still get credit as a sponsor. Choose wisely!

### Can I cancel a sponsorship?
Once approved and paid, it cannot be cancelled. The blockchain transaction is permanent.

### What if I don't know anyone to sponsor me?
Post in [Skatehive Discord](https://discord.gg/skatehive) #sponsorship channel! Active members often sponsor newcomers with good vibes.

### Do I get my HIVE back?
No, the 3 HIVE goes to the Hive blockchain network to create the account. It's a one-time cost.

### Can I sponsor using my Hive Power instead?
Yes! The RC Delegation method uses your Hive Power (Resource Credits) instead of liquid HIVE.

### What's the difference between sponsorship and invite?
- **Sponsorship:** You pay to create their Hive account
- **Invite:** You share a link for them to join with email (no cost)
