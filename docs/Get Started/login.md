# How to Login

### How to Login to Skatehive.app

Follow these steps to log in to **Skatehive** using **Hive Keychain**.

---

### Step 1: Visit Skatehive

Go to [**Skatehive**](https://skatehive.app/) and import your Hive account.

---

### Step 2: Use Hive Keychain

- Click on **"Login"**.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreig5xvxi6owgyi52skzn7opvlabbo6qakfiqartywp3h2vqqoz5hkq?filename=skatehive.png)

- Click on **"Connect"** via **Hive**.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreidicbn5ysffdgjsmv6yx3wsyhomf32us7v2kzxznl2zmoldxhnbge?filename=skatehive.png)

- Select **"Keychain"**.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreieaovrnvench5q6skrn34abrgxztv5cvcpnt2voqref6j6wxqzaii?filename=skatehive.png)

> **Note:** If you don’t have Hive Keychain installed, [**Download Hive Keychain**](https://hive-keychain.com/) and click on **"Login"**.

---

### Step 3: Enter Your Hive Username

Type your **Hive username** in the text box and click on the **"arrow"** to the right.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreibz2ttvpgg6oupkmvnrnj2t2oedydbnbebc5ixfglseww2k3p2gvq?filename=skatehive.png)

---

### Step 4: Confirm the Keychain Transaction

Approve the **Keychain message** to proceed.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreif7bvevx5cg2vs6xanjqmdshzhropjmgza2b4xe2sozge2gevvwd4?filename=skatehive.png)

---

### Step 5: Welcome to Skatehive!

You’re all set! Explore and enjoy **Skatehive**.

![](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/bafkreicrzxna3olxnntfcofh2z3zqzmlou7mi7e6hy2lz25lkgi553w4au?filename=skatehive.png)
