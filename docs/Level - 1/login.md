# How to Log in to Skatehive.app

Follow the steps below to log in to **Skatehive** using **Hive Keychain**.

## **Step 1:** Go to [Skatehive.app](https://skatehive.app/) and click on **"Login"**.

## **Step 2:** Click on **"Login with Keychain"**.
