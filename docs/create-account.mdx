# **How to Create a Skatehive Account** üõπ

Skatehive offers multiple ways to create an account, whether you're inviting a friend or setting one up yourself. Follow the steps below to get started.

---

## **1. Invite a Friend to Join Skatehive**

You can refer a friend and help them create an account directly through your own Skatehive profile.

### **Steps to Invite a Friend**

1. **Go to the robot icon** at the top of the Skatehive platform or [click here to get started](https://skatehive.app).  
   <img src="https://i.ibb.co/JCCj9Sf/image.png" alt="Robot Icon" width="150" />

2. **Choose a username and email for your friend.**  
   Ensure they are happy with the username, as it cannot be easily changed. Type in their email address.  
   ![Enter Username and Email](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/H1tZGNP1Jx.png)

3. Click **"Looks Good, Go For It"**.  
   A Hive Keychain transaction will be prompted for approval. Once approved, the account will be created, and your friend will receive an email.

---

## **2. Receiving the Email Invite**

Once the account is created, your friend will receive an email containing their **HIVE KEYS**.  
![Hive Keys Email](https://ipfs.skatehive.app/ipfs/QmbTbULhRtAfyd19cTNYfrsjpsuRN2TV9sxZ5yzQQeR44D)

### **Important: Keep Your Keys Safe**
- **Your Hive keys are irreplaceable.** If lost, they cannot be recovered.  
- Skatehive.app does not store your keys for security purposes‚Äîit‚Äôs your responsibility to keep them secure.

## **4. Setting Up Hive Keychain**

Hive Keychain is a secure tool to store your private keys and interact with Hive-based platforms, including Skatehive. It‚Äôs available as a browser extension for desktop or as a mobile app (which also serves as a web browser).

### **Steps for Mobile Users**
1. Open Hive Keychain and set a secure PIN to unlock it.  
   ![Set PIN](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/rk9Y1SDJJg.png)

2. Click **Use Keys/Password** and enter your **Master Password** to add your account.  
   ![Enter Master Password](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/HyBYyBwJJx.png)

3. Log in to Skatehive by entering your username.  
   ![Login to Skatehive](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/ByGmlHDkJl.png)

Once your transaction is approved, you‚Äôll be logged in.

---

## **5. Creating a Hive Account Yourself**

If you prefer to create a Hive account on your own, you can do so through other Hive frontends. Once created, you can use this account to log into Skatehive.

### **Steps to Create a Hive Account**
1. Ensure you have the **Hive Keychain** extension installed.
2. Visit [HiveOnboard](https://hiveonboard.com/create-account) and follow the instructions to create a Hive account.

Once your Hive account is set up, you can use it to log into Skatehive and begin exploring the community.
