## Option 2: Create an Invite

If you‚Äôre already a Skatehive member, you can invite friends to join.

1. **Click the Invite icon** on the top bar of [skatehive.app](https://skatehive.app) or go directly to [skatehive.app/invite](https://www.skatehive.app/invite)
2. Choose a **username** and enter their **email address**

### Set Up Keychain on Mobile

1. Create a PIN to unlock the app  
   ![Set PIN](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/rk9Y1SDJJg.png)

2. Tap **Use Keys/Password** and enter your **Master Password**  
   ![Enter Master Password](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/HyBYyBwJJx.png)

3. Enter your **username** to log into Skatehive  
   ![Login to Skatehive](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/ByGmlHDkJl.png)

After approval in Keychain, you're in.
